  • How do I change my Password?

    • If you are logged into online classroom account, you can change your password from your Settings. From here
    1. Click password.
    2. Type your current and new password.
    3. Click save changes.

  • How do I sign up?

    • If you don’t have your online classroom account, you can sign up for one in a few steps:

    1. Go to online classroom’s login page.
    2. You will see the signup form, then fill up your name, email address, password.
    3. Click signup.

  • Where are my privacy settings?

    • After logging you account, you can find your privacy option from your setting.

  • How can I deactivate my account?

    • To deactivate your account:

    1. Select settings.
    2. Choose deactivate your account. Then follow the steps.

  • Who can see my messages?

    • Only you and the people are you messaging can view the contents and history of your conservation.Each of your conversations is separate. Even if some of the same people are in more than one conversation, we don't combine those conversations into one thread.

  • How do I turn in my homework?

    • After logging your account, go to Classes. Then choose upload. Select the file you want to turn in.

  • How do I edit basic info on my profile?

    • To edit your basic personal info (ex: gender, contact info, id, and department)

    1. Go to your profile
    2. Choose Settings of your menu bar.
    3. Click the section you'd like to change.
    4. Click save changes.

  • How do I reset my password?

    • If you can't log into online classroom and need to reset your password:

    1. Go to the login page
    2. Click forgot password?
    3. Type the email, phone number, full name or username associated with your Account, then click Search and follow the on-screen instructions
